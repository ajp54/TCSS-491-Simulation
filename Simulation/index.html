How The Simulation Works:

Green Cells: These are food for the non-red cells. A new green cell is generated every 0.5 seconds and there is a small chance that a newly spawned green cell will mutate into a white cell.
Red Cells: These are the preditor cells. They hunt the non-green cells for food but can die if they do not eat anything for long enough. if a red cell eats enough other cells it will produce a new red cell.
White Cells: These cells eat the green cells and run from the red cells. If a white cell eats enough food, it will produce a new cell. Anytime a new cell is produced, it has a small chance of mutating into a cell with slightly different attributes.
Other Colored Cells: a cell that is any other color is one that has mutated from a white cell. The attributes that change include the cell's speed, color, size, range at which is can see other cells, and number of children it reproduces at a time.
